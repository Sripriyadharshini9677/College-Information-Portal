Well-equipped Laboratories

       

Our laboratories are equipped with modern instruments and technology, allowing students to conduct experiments and gain hands-on experience across various disciplines. Safety protocols and regular maintenance ensure a high-quality learning environment.        

       

Modern Library

       

The library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, digital resources, and research papers. With comfortable reading areas and computer access, students can study, research, and explore a wide array of academic materials.        

       

Sports Complex

       

Our campus features a well-maintained sports complex that includes facilities for indoor and outdoor games such as basketball, volleyball, cricket, and badminton. Regular sports events and fitness sessions promote student health and teamwork.        

       

WiFi Enabled Campus

       

The entire campus is WiFi-enabled, ensuring that students and faculty have access to high-speed internet for academic research, e-learning, and communication. Connectivity is maintained across classrooms, labs, hostels, and open spaces.        

       

Auditorium

       

The auditorium is a state-of-the-art facility used for seminars, guest lectures, cultural events, and student presentations. It is fully air-conditioned with advanced lighting and sound systems to support all types of programs and gatherings.
